🛡️ How to Spot Phishing Emails

🚩 Red Flags

⚠️ Urgent or threatening language ("Act now!")
⚠️ Suspicious sender email address
⚠️ Generic greetings ("Dear Customer")
⚠️ Unexpected attachments or links
⚠️ Requests for passwords or personal data

✅ What To Do

✅ Hover over links to check the real URL
✅ Verify sender identity independently
✅ Report suspicious emails to IT Security
✅ Never share credentials via email
✅ When in doubt, call the sender directly
